Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a recognized name in streetwear fashion.

Since 2016

Chitahela was founded in 2016 with a vision to redefine and promote authentic African streetwear, blending urban aesthetics with premium quality materials and craftsmanship.

Our journey began with a big dream. Today, we've grown into a recognized brand delivering our bold designs across Africa, with a dedicated service throughout South Africa.

Operating as a cohesive remote team, every garment we create tells a story of passion, dedication, and the relentless pursuit of excellence, connecting digitally to serve the continent.
